VERNON TENNIS ASSOCIATION

JUNIOR DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM LESSONS

SUMMER 2025

We are pleased to once again offer lessons for our Vernon youth in July and August. The focus of the summer program is on skill development and learning to play tennis. Tournaments will be set up to give players the opportunity to play. More information about the planned tournament will follow.

SUMMER PROGRAM INFORMATION

Cost

The cost for this program is:

For players registered with the VTA spring program

  • $45 for the 5 one hour lessons for red ball and orange ball.
  • $70 for the 5 one and a half hour lessons for green dot and regular ball players.

NOTE – As players need to be registered with the VTA, there is a $10 fee to cover insurance and club membership with Tennis BC. This enables our association to partake in Tennis BC programs and activities.

Payment for the lessons.

Payment for lessons will not be required until a week prior to the lesson commencing. Direction for the payment will be given at that time.
